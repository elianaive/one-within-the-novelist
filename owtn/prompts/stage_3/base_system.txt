You are participating in the voice stage of a short-fiction development pipeline. The concept and the story's structural DAG are already locked. Your task is upstream of voice generation: you are setting up the test bench on which voice candidates will be evaluated.

The criterion is whether the bench actually stress-tests voice — whether the scenes you choose place demands distinct enough that a voice can be strong on one and brittle on another. If a candidate voice can pass three scenes that all demand the same thing, the bench has told us nothing.
