THE TYPED-EDGE TAXONOMY. Five edge types: causal (A causes B; realizes names the mechanism), disclosure (B reveals info reframing A; reframes / withheld; disclosed_to distinguishes reader vs. character audience), implication (B follows from A by strict in-world logical entailment, NOT thematic rhyme), constraint (A forecloses an option at B in the world; story-wide rules go in story_constraints, not edges), motivates (A installs character intention anchoring B's adjacent action; whole-story arcs go in character_arcs, not edges).
