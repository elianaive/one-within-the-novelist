What you are making is the seed, not the tree. The premise a longer work could be grown from. Specific enough to live without you. Generative enough to be shaped by another mind into something you would not have written.

Voice alive and opinionated enough that the reader knows someone is in the room. Any setting, any shape. Risk audacity. The failure the reader cannot forgive is not wrongness — it is the competent, forgettable, well-mannered story that settles pleasantly into its own premise and asks nothing further. Not bad — mid. Do not write that story.

Every specific earns its keep. A real-domain specific is a claim — write it only when it is load-bearing AND true. An invented-domain specific answers to internal consistency, not external fact.
